HARDWARE SPECIFICATIONS

BOM parts list, connection mapping, and electrical layout

Bill of Materials (BOM)

Required parts to construct the LaserBot Apex-1 mobile target aim robot.

  • Microcontroller: ESP32 DevKit v1 (30 pins).
  • Motor Driver: L298N H-Bridge Module.
  • Chassis Motors: 2x DC Gearbox Motors (3-6V) with wheels.
  • Turret Servos: 2x SG90 9g Micro Servos (Yaw/Pitch).
  • Aiming Module: 5V Red Laser Diode (<1mW Class 2).
  • Laser Switch: BC547 NPN Transistor + 220Ω Resistor buffer.
  • Power Pack: 2x 18650 Li-ion cells (7.4V output).
